Ernakulam

Explore the beauty and attractions of Ernakulam.

Ernakulam,the commercial hub of Kerala, is a dynamic city that seamlessly blends modernity with historical charm. Boasting a cosmopolitan atmosphere, it is home to iconic landmarks like the Marine Drive, showcasing a picturesque waterfront. The city's rich history is evident in structures such as the historic Mattancherry Palace and the Paradesi Synagogue, reflecting influences of Portuguese, Dutch, and Jewish cultures. With its bustling markets, thriving industries, and a backdrop of serene backwaters, Ernakulam captures the essence of Kerala's urban and cultural diversity.
