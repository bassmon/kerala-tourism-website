Idukki

Explore the beauty and attractions of Idukki.

Idukki,a captivating district in Kerala, is a treasure trove of natural beauty and serenity. Nestled within the Western Ghats, it is renowned for the impressive Idukki Dam, an engineering marvel that harnesses the power of Periyar River. The district's landscape is adorned with lush tea plantations, dense forests, and cascading waterfalls like the enchanting Thommankuthu. Idukki offers a tranquil escape into nature, making it a haven for trekking, wildlife enthusiasts, and those seeking respite amidst the breathtaking hills of Kerala.
