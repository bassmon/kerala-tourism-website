Thiruvanathapuram

Explore the beauty and attractions of Thiruvanathapuram.

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala, is a captivating blend of tradition, spirituality, and coastal allure. Home to revered temples like the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, it carries a rich cultural legacy. The regal Kowdiar Palace and Napier Museum showcase its colonial history. Serene beaches like Kovalam add a coastal charm, making it a city where historic landmarks, vibrant markets, and the natural beauty of the Arabian Sea converge seamlessly. Thiruvananthapuram is a city of contrasts, inviting exploration into its religious heritage, architectural marvels, and the tranquil embrace of coastal landscapes.
