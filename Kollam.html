Kollam

Explore the beauty and attractions of Kollam.

Kollam, a coastal city in Kerala, beckons with a unique blend of history and natural splendor. The city's historic significance is evident in remnants of Portuguese and Dutch influence, including the imposing Thangassery Lighthouse and the picturesque Ashtamudi Lake. Kollam is a gateway to the scenic backwaters, offering enchanting houseboat cruises and serene landscapes. With vibrant markets like the historic Chinnakada, a bustling trade scene, and tranquil beaches, Kollam invites visitors to experience the timeless charm of coastal Kerala.
